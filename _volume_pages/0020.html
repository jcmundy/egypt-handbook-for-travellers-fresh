{% raw %}EauiPMENT. F'or all ordinary purposes a couple of light tweed{% endraw %}
{% raw %}suits, a few flannel and soft cotton shirts, a supply of thin woollen{% endraw %}
{% raw %}socks, one pair of light and easy boots, one of shoes, and one of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}slippers, a moderately warm ulster or long travelling cloak, a pith-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}helmet and a soft felt hat, together with the most necessary articles{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of the toilet, will amply suffice. It is advisable, for the prevention{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of colds and chills, to wear a woollen fabric next the skin; but{% endraw %}
{% raw %}light underclothing, with an Oxford shirt, will be found more{% endraw %}
{% raw %}suitable to the climate than a heavy flannel shirt. Evening dress is{% endraw %}
{% raw %}usually worn at dinner at the principal hotels. A light silken (or{% endraw %}
{% raw %}muslin) cloth tied round the hat and allowed to fall over the back{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of the neck and ears is an indispensable protection against the sun.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}In prolonged riding tours a sun-shade is a fatiguing encumbrance.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}All articles should be new and strongly made, as it is often difficult{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to get repairs properly executed in Egypt. Few travellers walk in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Egypt, except for very short distances, but sportsmen should add a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}stout pair of waterproof shooting-boots to their equipment.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Among the most important extras to be brought from Europe are a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}drinking cup of leather or metal, a flask, a strong pocket-knife, a thermo¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}meter, a pocket-compass of medium size, and an acetylene lamp for light¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ing caverns and dark chambers. — Photographic materials, dry-plates,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}films, etc., can be obtained in Cairo, but it is preferable to bring a good{% endraw %}
{% raw %}stock carefully packed from home, taking care to attend the customs{% endraw %}
{% raw %}examination in person. The plates should not be more than 8 by 10 inches{% endraw %}
{% raw %}at the largest.{% endraw %}
