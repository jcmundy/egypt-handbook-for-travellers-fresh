{% raw %}(10). Egypt as a Health Resort. Medical Hints.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The beneficial influence of the climate of Egypt has been known{% endraw %}
{% raw %}since the Roman period at least, and of late years an increasing{% endraw %}
{% raw %}number of visitors have flocked to the Nile to enjoy the benefits{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of its remarkably dry winter-climate. Phthisis (in certain cases),{% endraw %}
{% raw %}asthma, chronic bronchitis, Bright's disease, rheumatoid arthritis,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}gout, insomnia, dyspepsia, and mental strain are some of the most{% endraw %}
{% raw %}important ailments that are at least alleviated by a visit to Egypt.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}In deciding which ofthe health-resorts in Egypt a given case should{% endraw %}
{% raw %}he sent to, the physician must of course consider whether or not{% endraw %}
{% raw %}warmth must be secured along with dryness of air, whether purity{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of air alone or also a bright stimulating climate is to he specially{% endraw %}
{% raw %}sought, and whether cold winds are harmful or not. Invalids who{% endraw %}
{% raw %}have not been ordered to a particular health-resort before leaving{% endraw %}
{% raw %}home should consult a physician immediately on arriving in Egypt;{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and it is advisable in all cases to secure the advice of the physician{% endraw %}
{% raw %}resident at the spot selected.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}It is now generally understood that Cairo cannot properly be con¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}sidered a health-resort. The presence of a large city with its noise{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and bustle, its situation, exposed to the influence of a wind coming{% endraw %}
{% raw %}from the N. over the broad cultivated Delta, the unhealthy saturation{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in the early part of winter of considerable low-lying tracts of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}town composed of old rubbish, and the higher relative humidity, all{% endraw %}
{% raw %}combine to compel those who seek health from the climate of Egypt{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to look to other station,?. There are, however, excellent health-re¬{% endraw %}
