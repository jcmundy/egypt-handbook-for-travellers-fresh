{% raw %}The beginning of anything like a regular political development{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in Egypt cannot be dated before the introduction of agriculture;{% endraw %}
{% raw %}most probably it began with the cultivation of wheat and barley,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}grains of which have been found among the remains in the most{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ancient Egyptian tombs, dating from before the earliest dynasty.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The origin of both these cereals is indisputably Asiatic; their first{% endraw %}
{% raw %}home was in the valley of the Euphrates or in some more central{% endraw %}
{% raw %}region of the continent. Besides these grains the funeral offer¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ings under the earliest dynasties included also linen, wine, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the produce of other cultivated plants, originally indigenous to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}W. Asia.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Some of the earliest ideal conceptions of the proto-Egyptians{% endraw %}
{% raw %}must also have been drawn from Asiatic sources, which, however,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in this case are to be looked for farther to the S. in that continent.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Not only the use of incense but also the sycamore and the persea,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the two sacred trees in the Egyptian Pantheon, were known in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Egypt from the very earliest period. But all these plants are ex¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}clusively indigenous to the mountainous regions of S. Arabia and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the adjoining coasts ofthe Red Sea; they could have been derived{% endraw %}
{% raw %}from no other source. The use of incense is as ancient as the most{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ancient known religion. The tree called by the Greeks persea, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}known to modern botanists as mimusops, flourished in the gardens{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of ancient Egypt; and the sycamore, which is now nowhere found{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in a wild state outside the regions mentioned above, is to be seen{% endraw %}
{% raw %}all over Egypt at the present day.{% endraw %}
