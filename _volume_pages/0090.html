{% raw %}V. DYNASTY.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Egypt now reached the zenith of her civilization; art, in particular,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}attained a perfection never again reached. The pyramids of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}kings are mostly near Abusir (p. 129), where also special sanctuaries{% endraw %}

{% raw %}Sakkara (p. 134). After his death internal dissensions seem to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}have broken out, resulting in the accession of a new dynasty.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}VI. DYNASTY.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Under this dynasty the power of the kings was more limited, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the small principalities recovered some of their independence. In{% endraw %}
{% raw %}foreign affairs far-reaching commercial relations were entered into{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with Central Africa, Punt (the S. coast of the Red Sea), Syria, etc.{% endraw %}
