{% raw %}teira. Memphites, a son of Euergetes, became, under the{% endraw %}

{% raw %}death the government was shared by his widow —{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Cleopatra Cocce and her son Ptolemy X. Soter II. (Lathyrus).{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Soter II. was banished, and his brother Ptolemy XI. Alex¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ander I. became co-regent in his stead.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Alexander, expelled by a rebellion, perished in a naval{% endraw %}
{% raw %}battle. Soter II. was recalled.{% endraw %}

{% raw %}married Cleopatra Berenice, with whom he reigned jointly.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}He assassinated his wife and was himself slain.{% endraw %}

{% raw %}the Roman Senate. Pompey was appointed guardian.{% endraw %}

{% raw %}defeated at the battle of Pharsalia, sought refuge in Egypt,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}but on landing was slain at the instigation of Ptolemy,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}his ward.{% endraw %}
