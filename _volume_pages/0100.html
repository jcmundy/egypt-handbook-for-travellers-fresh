{% raw %}The Roman emperors followed the example of the Ptolemies{% endraw %}

{% raw %}of the old Pharaohs and in maintaining the appearance of a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}national Egyptian state. — Christianity was early introduced{% endraw %}

{% raw %}Cornelius Gallus, the first prefect, repressed an insurrection{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in Upper Egypt and fought against the Ethiopians. Hav¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ing afterwards fallen into disgrace with the emperor, he{% endraw %}
{% raw %}committed suicide. — The reformed calendar was finally{% endraw %}
{% raw %}introduced by Augustus.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Caesar Octavianus, under the title of Augustus, became{% endraw %}
{% raw %}sole ruler ofthe vast Roman empire (p. 8).{% endraw %}
