{% raw %}pelled by the insurgents to capitulate at Damascus, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}was executed (May, 1412).{% endraw %}
{% raw %}El-Mahmudi Muaiyad (p. 47) succeeded Farag. His reign{% endraw %}
{% raw %}was chiefly occupied with victorious campaigns against his{% endraw %}
{% raw %}unruly Syrian vassals, in which he was greatly aided by{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the military talents of his son Ibrahim.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}He exacted heavy contributions from Christians and Jews,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and he re-enacted and rigorously enforced the sumptuary laws{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of 'Omar, Mutawakkil, Hakim, and Mohammed en-Nasir. Not{% endraw %}
{% raw %}only were the colours to be worn by the Christians and Jews{% endraw %}
{% raw %}prescribed (the costume of the former being dark-blue, with{% endraw %}
{% raw %}black turbans, and a wooden cross weighing 51bs. hung round{% endraw %}
{% raw %}their necks; that of the latter, yellow, with black turbans,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and a black ball hung from their necks); but the fashion of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}their dress and length of their turbans, and even the costume{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of their women, were so regulated as entirely to distinguish{% endraw %}
{% raw %}them from the followers of the prophet.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Bursbey (Berisbai; p. 66), who had for a time been the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}vicegerent of an infant sultan, ascended the throne on April{% endraw %}
{% raw %}1st, 1422. He waged successful campaigns against Cyprus{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and the Mongols.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Kait Bey (pp. 55, 66) was one of the last independent{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Mameluke sultans of Egypt. Both as a general and a diplo¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}matist he successfully maintained his position against{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the Turks (Sultans Mohammed and Bajazid), and even{% endraw %}
{% raw %}inflicted serious losses on them ; but the refractory Mame¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}lukes obstructed his undertakings and in 1496 compelled{% endraw %}
{% raw %}him to abdicate in favour of his son Mohammed, a hoy of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}fourteen.{% endraw %}
