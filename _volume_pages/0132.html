{% raw %}characteristics, prevailed in other cases from a comparatively early{% endraw %}

{% raw %}farther to the S. and Thebes became the capital in place of Mem¬{% endraw %}

{% raw %}increased. At the beginning of the New Empire he was the head{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of the Egyptian pantheon. The great campaigns against Nubia and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Asia were waged in his name by the Theban kings, temples were{% endraw %}
{% raw %}erected to him in the conquered lands, and the lion's share of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}spoil fell to his shrines in Egypt, especially to the temple at{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Thebes. Ammon, in short, became the national god, the success¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ful rival of his predecessor Re-Harmachis. It was not to be ex¬{% endraw %}
