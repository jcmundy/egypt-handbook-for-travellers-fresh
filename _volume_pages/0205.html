{% raw %}Arrival at Alexandria (comp. Map, p. 18). The perfectly{% endraw %}
{% raw %}flat N.E. coast of Egypt, and even Alexandria itself, are not visible{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to the steamboat-passenger until very shortly before the vessel enters{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the harbour. We first observe the lighthouse. The steamer takes a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}pilot on board and is steered by him through a narrow, shallow, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}rocky channel (Boghazj into the harbour. As the passage can only{% endraw %}
{% raw %}be effected by daylight, vessels arriving in the evening must ride at{% endraw %}
{% raw %}anchor outside until next morning. To the left, on the Rds et-Tln{% endraw %}
{% raw %}('Cape of Figs'), are the viceroy's palace and the arsenal. Most of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the steamers lie alongside the quays.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}As soon as the brief sanitary inspection is over, the porters and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}commissionnaires swarm wildly on deck, vociferating in half-a-dozen{% endraw %}
{% raw %}different languages, and with animated gesticulations precipitate{% endraw %}
{% raw %}themselves upon the travellers' luggage. The best plan, especial¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ly when ladies are of the party, is to secure the services of one of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Cook's or Gaze's agents, who relieves the traveller of all trouble,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}assists in clearing luggage at the custom-house, and conducts the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}travellers to the special omnibuses for the hotels or railway-station.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}These agents are recognizable by the official caps, while the Arabs{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in the service of these firms bear large brass plates on their breasts.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}A sum of 15-20 pias. generally covers the total expenses of landing,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}etc., but a great deal of trouble is saved by procuring a landing{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ticket before leaving home. — The Custom House Examination,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}which takes place on shore, resembles the process in Europe.{% endraw %}
