{% raw %}Room III. "Enamelled Hanging Lamps from Mosques; most of them{% endraw %}
{% raw %}made of common green glass, with enamelled inscriptions, medallions,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}coats - of - arms, etc. These were used as ornaments, not for lighting{% endraw %}
{% raw %}purposes, and are seldom older than the 14th century. The place of man¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ufacture is unknown. Only about a hundred of these lamps are now extant,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and most of them (over 60) are in this museum. About 25, or a fourth{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of the total, are from the mosque of Sultan Hasan.{% endraw %}
