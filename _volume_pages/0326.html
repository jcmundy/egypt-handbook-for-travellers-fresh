{% raw %}7. Outer Environs of Cairo to the North and East.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}As the obelisk at Heliopolis is the only one now standing in Lower{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Egypt on its original site, since the removal of Cleopatra's Needle (p. 18),{% endraw %}
{% raw %}it ought to be visited by travellers who do not intend to ascend the Nile.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}— The view from the Mokattam Hills ranks with Karnak and Philae among{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the most beautiful sights'in Egypt; and only very blase' travellers will be{% endraw %}
{% raw %}content merely with the views from the Windmill Hill (p. 67) and the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Citadel (p. 51). — The Petrified Forest is, of course, especially interesting{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to geologists ; but even' ordinary travellers will find their account in an{% endraw %}
{% raw %}expedition to it, especially if they have not previously seen the desert. —{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The Barrage du Nil is the largest erection of the kind in the world and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}is interesting as such not only to engineers. It will, however, he sur¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}passed in size by the new dam at Assuan (p. 335).{% endraw %}
{% raw %}1. Heliopolis.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}This expedition is best made by Carriage (p. 27; drive to the obelisk{% endraw %}
{% raw %}l'/a hr.), though it may also be accomplished by Railway, starting from{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the small Matariyeh Station (PL B, 1; p. 24). Trains run hourly.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The High Road leads through the 'Abbasiyeh (p. 77) and past{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Kubbeh and the Khedivial Palace (Palais Taufik; p. 77). The plain{% endraw %}
{% raw %}between Kubbeh and Matariyeh has been the scene of two important{% endraw %}
{% raw %}battles. In 1517 the Battle of Heliopolis made Selim and the Turks{% endraw %}
{% raw %}masters of Egypt; and on 20th March, 1800, General Kleber with{% endraw %}
{% raw %}10,000 French troops succeeded in defeating 60,000 Orientals, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in consequence of this victory regained possession of Cairo, although{% endraw %}
{% raw %}for a short time only. At a little distance to the right and, farther{% endraw %}
{% raw %}on, also to the left, numerous villas have been built within the last{% endraw %}
{% raw %}few years. The drive to Matariyeh takes about il/-2 hr.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The Railway passes the following stations : l3/,j M. Demirddsh{% endraw %}
{% raw %}(Demerdache), station for the 'Abbasiyeh (p. 77); 2'/2 M. Manshiet-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}el-Sadr; 3 M. Kubri Kubbeh (bridge); 3^2 M. Hammdmdt Kubbeh{% endraw %}
{% raw %}(Koubbeh-les-bains); 474 M. Serai Kubbeh (Palais de Koubbeh;{% endraw %}
{% raw %}viceregal palace, p. 77); 5 M. Ezbet ez-ZeitUn, with numerous{% endraw %}
{% raw %}villas. — 6 M. Matariyeh, station for Heliopolis. (The railway goes{% endraw %}
{% raw %}onto El-Merg, p. 108.){% endraw %}
{% raw %}Near the insignificant village of Matariyeh are the Tree andWell{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of the Virgin and the Obelisk of Heliopolis. The Virgin's Tree (in a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}garden to the right of the road) is a sycamore with a decayed and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}riven trunk, covered with names and inscriptions, but the branches{% endraw %}
{% raw %}are still tolerably flourishing. According to the legend, the Virgin{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and Child once rested under the shade of this tree during the Flight{% endraw %}
{% raw %}into Egypt; and there is another tradition to the effect that the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}persecuted Mary concealed herself with the Child in a hollow of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the trunk, and that a spider so completely covered the opening{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with its web as to screen her effectually from observation. The{% endraw %}
{% raw %}present tree, the predecessor of which died in 1665, was not{% endraw %}
{% raw %}planted till after 1672; it is now protected by a railing. The garden{% endraw %}
{% raw %}is watered by means of a double sakiyeh, which is supplied from a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}shallow reservoir fed by springs. This water is good for drinking,{% endraw %}
