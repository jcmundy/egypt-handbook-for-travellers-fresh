{% raw %}The Branch Railway to the Barrage is traversed by four trains daily{% endraw %}
{% raw %}from Cairo (15 M., in about 1 hr.; fare 12 or 6 pias., return, 18 or 9 pias.).{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The intermediate stations are Shubra and Kalyub. Opposite the Barrage{% endraw %}
{% raw %}station is a small restaurant, where luncheon may be obtained if required.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Donkeys may be hired at the station. — A narrow-gauge line, worked by{% endraw %}
{% raw %}hand-power and also available for passengers, unites Barrage with the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}station of El-Mendshi, on the West Nile Railway (p. 23). — Messrs. Cook{% endraw %}
{% raw %}& Son arrange special excursions by steam-launch twice a week, or oftener{% endraw %}
