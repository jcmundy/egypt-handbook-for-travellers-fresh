{% raw %}Tramway from the Great Nile Bridge at Kasr en-Nil (p. 75) to the Mena{% endraw %}

{% raw %}sun, especially on the top of the Great Pyramid. Ladies who intend to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ascend the pyramids should dress accordingly. A repetition of the ex¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}cursion by moonlight produces an ineffaceable impression (electric tram¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}way at about 9 and 10 on moonlight nights).{% endraw %}
