{% raw %}The first tomb on the left in the Street of Tombs proper is that of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}"Enkh-me-Hor, surnamed Sesi. Through the door we enter a chamber{% endraw %}

{% raw %}inspecting his fisheries. In one of the doorways are interesting reliefs of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}surgical operations; on the right, circumcision, on the left, an operation{% endraw %}
{% raw %}on a man's toe. In the adjoining room are figures of dancers. — The{% endraw %}

{% raw %}'the first next to the king'. In a chamber with fine painted reliefs is the{% endraw %}
