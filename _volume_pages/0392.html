{% raw %}Railway to (14 M.) Helwan via, Turra in 25-45 min.; trains hourly to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and from Cairo (fares and times to be learned at the hotels).{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The trains start from the station in the Bdb el-Luk Square{% endraw %}
{% raw %}(PI. B, 5), and follow the direction of the Shari'a el-Mansur. Beyond{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the stations of Seyideh Zenab (PI. B, 7), Fum el-Khallg (p. 68), and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}St. Georges (p. 73), the.railway traverses the narrow plain on the E.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}bank of the Nile, generally on the boundary between the cultivated{% endraw %}
{% raw %}land and the desert. — After two more stoppages it reaches Turra{% endraw %}
{% raw %}or Tourah. A little to the right are the large military establishments{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and gunpowder mills and about l1^ M. to the left are the quarries{% endraw %}

{% raw %}neighbourhood, known as 'Balat', and used for flooring purposes in{% endraw %}
