{% raw %}covered with glazed mosaic tiles, most of which are now in the Gizeh{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Museum. These tiles are of special technical interest, as their colouring{% endraw %}

{% raw %}Jerusalem by the Syrian party, and had met with a hospitable reception{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in Egypt. A visit to the ruins scarcely repays the trouble, as most of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}them are again buried in rubbish.{% endraw %}
