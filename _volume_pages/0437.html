{% raw %}Dahabiyehs. — Now that the visit to Upper Egypt is so much{% endraw %}
{% raw %}facilitated by the railway and the regular services of tourist-steam¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ers, only those travellers to whom economy of time and money is{% endraw %}
{% raw %}no object make the voyage in privately hired dahabiyehs or launches.{% endraw %}

{% raw %}fitted up, are those belonging to Messrs. Cook <y Son (4 steam-daha-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}biyehs, 6 sailing dahabiyehs) and the Anglo-American Co. (3 large{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and 2 small steam-dahabiyehs, several sailing dahabiyehs). The{% endraw %}
