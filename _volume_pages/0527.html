{% raw %}of Amenophis III., Tut-enkh-Amon, and Eye). The left or N. tower{% endraw %}

{% raw %}figure on the right side still stands and displays excellent work¬{% endraw %}

{% raw %}the cartouches of Ramses I., Sethos I., and Ramses II. are found, an{% endraw %}
