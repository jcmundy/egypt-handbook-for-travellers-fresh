{% raw %}entrance. Above the door are Isis and Nephthys, worshipping the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}solar disk, in which stand the ram-headed sun-god and a scarabseus.{% endraw %}

{% raw %}in prayer; an inscription indicates one of these as 'Apa Ammonios,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the martyr'.{% endraw %}
