{% raw %}At the left (S.) end of the Punt Colonnade is a curious little{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Shrine op Hathor, goddess of the dead (p. cxxvi), which was also{% endraw %}
{% raw %}reached from below by a flight of steps. The innermost chambers,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}which are hewn in the rock, are preceded by two covered Colonnades{% endraw %}
{% raw %}(now in ruins). The first of these had sixteen-sided columns and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}square pillars with Hathor- capitals; the second, which lay at a slightly{% endraw %}
{% raw %}higher level, had four columns with round shafts (three still stand¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ing) and also sixteen-sided columns (six partly remaining).{% endraw %}
