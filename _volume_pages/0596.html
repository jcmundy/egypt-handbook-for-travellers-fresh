{% raw %}second court. The temple itself, to the left, comprises a Cella{% endraw %}

{% raw %}a parapet upon which rise square pillars, which support the roof{% endraw %}
{% raw %}along with the sixteen-sided columns in the inner row. The re¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}presentations show Thutmosis III. sacrificing or performing other{% endraw %}
{% raw %}sacred rites in presence of the gods. In the inner chambers Thut¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}mosis I. and Thutmosis II. also appear in place of the original{% endraw %}
{% raw %}figures of Makere. The inscriptions on the pillars on each side of{% endraw %}
