{% raw %}The Great Hypostyle Hall (Pronaos) contains 10 columns with{% endraw %}

{% raw %}designs (star-gods in their boats, etc.). We may observe the lines{% endraw %}
{% raw %}dividing the surface into squares to guide the artist in drawing the{% endraw %}
