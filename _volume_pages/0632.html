{% raw %}The Convent of St. Simeon (Der Anba Sama'dn) is situated on{% endraw %}
{% raw %}a hill in the desert on the W. bank of the Nile. Landing opposite{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the S. end of Elephantine, we follow a desert-valley, passing several{% endraw %}
{% raw %}rock-tombs, and in 20 min. reach our goal. This is one of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}largest and best preserved Coptic convents, though it has been{% endraw %}
{% raw %}abandoned by the monks since the 13th century. Nothing certain{% endraw %}
{% raw %}is known as to the date of its origin. It stands surrounded by a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}wall, over 20 ft. high, the lower part of which is built of hewn{% endraw %}
{% raw %}stone, the upper part of bricks of Nile-mud. The two-storied Main{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Building lies to the N. The lower story contains a large central{% endraw %}
{% raw %}vaulted corridor, with cells opening off it on each side. On the E.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}wall of the corridor is a painting (only half preserved) of Christ{% endraw %}
{% raw %}enthroned, with the Archangel Michael and six Apostles beside him.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Each ofthe small cells contained six or eight beds, some of which{% endraw %}
{% raw %}still remain. At the N.W. angle is the refectory. The staircase is{% endraw %}
{% raw %}continued in the S. angle to the next story, which is similarly{% endraw %}
{% raw %}arranged, and thence to the roof. — Below the main building are{% endraw %}
{% raw %}several rock-hewn cells and a rock-chapel, with a tastefully painted{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ceiling and pictures of saints.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}To the S.E. of the main building, between it and the girdle{% endraw %}
{% raw %}wall, is the Church, comprising nave and aisles and a choir with{% endraw %}
{% raw %}sacristies on each side. The roof was vaulted throughout. In the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}semi-dome of the choir is a fine painting of Christ enthroned, be¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}tween four angels. At the W. end of the nave, opposite the choir,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}is a domed recess with a fresco of Christ with two angels bowing{% endraw %}
{% raw %}before him. There are numerous Coptic inscriptions in the sacristies.{% endraw %}
