{% raw %}Tickets of Admission to the antiquities, see p. 184. — As{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Shellal (Philae) is not yet united by railway with Wddi Haifa, visitors{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to Lower Nubia are dependent upon steamboats or dahabiyehs. The{% endraw %}
{% raw %}journey by land, on camels or donkeys, is fatiguing and by no{% endraw %}
{% raw %}means recommended.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}- Steamboats. — Cook's tourist-steamer Prince Abbas, starting{% endraw %}
{% raw %}every Monday from the end of December to the beginning of March,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}plies from Shellal to Wadi Haifa and back in 7 days (fare,incl. board,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}30i., for Cook's passengers 23J.). Though the steamer halts at a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}few points only, its passengers have an opportunity of securing a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}fairly adequate idea of the scenery and antiquities of Nubia.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The itinerary of this voyage is as follows: —{% endraw %}
{% raw %}1st Day (Monday). Start from Shellal (Phila?) at 10 a.m. Via{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Debot, Kertassi, Bab el-Kalabsheh, and Kalabsheh to Dendur.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Visit to the temple there.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}2nd Day. Via Gerf-Husen to Dakkeh, where the temple is{% endraw %}
{% raw %}visited ; thence to Sebu'a and Korosko.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}3rd Day. In the morning ascent of the Awas el-Guarani. The{% endraw %}
{% raw %}steamer starts at 10 a.m. for 'Amada (inspection of the temple),{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Derr, Ibrim, and Abu-Simbel.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}4th Day. From Abu-Simbel to Wadi Haifa.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}5th Day. Excursion by land to the Second Cataract. The steamer{% endraw %}
{% raw %}starts at noon for the return to Abu-Simbel, where the temples are{% endraw %}
{% raw %}visited.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}6th Day. Start at 10 a.m. from Abu-Simbel for Derr (short in¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}spection of the temple) and Maharaka (inspection of the temple).{% endraw %}
{% raw %}7th Day. Via Kalabsheh (inspection of the temple) to Shellal,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}arriving at 4 p.m., in time for the train to Assuan.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Goveknment Stbameks also ply between Shellal and Wadi Haifa.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}These vessels are fairly comfortable, but the rapidity of the voyage{% endraw %}
{% raw %}(80hrs. up, 30 hrs. down) leaves little time to visit the antiquities.{% endraw %}
